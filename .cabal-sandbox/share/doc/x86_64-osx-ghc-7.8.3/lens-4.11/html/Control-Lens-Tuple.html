Provides access to 1st field of a tuple.

Methods

_1 :: Lens s t a b

Access the 1st field of a tuple (and possibly change its type).

>>> (1,2)^._1
1

>>> _1 .~ "hello" $ (1,2)
("hello",2)

>>> (1,2) & _1 .~ "hello"
("hello",2)

>>> _1 putStrLn ("hello","world")
hello
((),"world")

This can also be used on larger tuples as well:

>>> (1,2,3,4,5) & _1 +~ 41
(42,2,3,4,5)

Methods

_2 :: Lens s t a b

Access the 2nd field of a tuple.

>>> _2 .~ "hello" $ (1,(),3,4)
(1,"hello",3,4)

>>> (1,2,3,4) & _2 *~ 3
(1,6,3,4)

>>> _2 print (1,2)
2
(1,())
